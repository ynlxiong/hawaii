Dress Code
Smart Casual — Hawaiian Style

Lightweight, breathable fabrics like linen or cotton work best. Go for pieces that feel relaxed but put-together — and wear something you'll love in photos!

Attire Ideas
  • Button-downs, breezy tops, or elegant blouses (florals and tropical prints welcome!)
  • Chinos, tailored shorts, flowy skirts, or relaxed-fit trousers
  • Sundresses, midi dresses, or coordinated sets for an easy, polished look
  • Footwear like loafers, clean sandals, wedges, or other comfortable dressy options
Color Palette

Whites, pastels, tropical prints, soft blues & greens — Hawaii colors look gorgeous in photos.

📸 There will be a photographer — wear something you love!
